About

Dogs love everybody, but everybody does not love dogs. Unfortunately, dogs are neglected by owners and even abandoned, forced to fend for themselves. Thankfully, there are many organizations that take these dogs into shelter and give them a safe space. We wanted to make a website that allowed dog adopters to easily search for dogs that are available for adoption through the PetFinder API. If dog adopters were unsure of the type of dogs they wanted, we displayed random pictures of dogs to help their decision process.
